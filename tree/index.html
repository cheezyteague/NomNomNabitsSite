    • About

      Nom Nom Nabits is an educational RPG Dungeon Crawler with Cyber-Security in mind. Play as one-of-four characters with the help of Dem, Disk, and VPN taking down evil-nabits and sabotaging hologhosts. Ignite and intrest in cybersecurity with concepts such as encryption, virtual private networks, and cookies while collecting keys to fight the boss and clues to finish the labs. Do you think you're able to become the ruler of the cyber world or will you fall to its inhabitants?

    Our Misson

    The ability to educate effectively relies on the accuracy of the information provided; without it our goal is sabotaged. To mitigate, we work tirelessly to triple-check the information provided and confirm any information with reputable spources.

    Without the engagement and involvement of the individual, education is not possible. We took time to craft enthrawling experiences to ignite an interest in Cyber-Security by getting the individual involved.

    Education is the ultimate goal. In a time where tech rules the world and development is happening at a rapid rate it is important to understand the risks and vulnerabilities that come along with it. Hopefully, through our project, individuals gain greater confidence that they can navigate this world safely.
